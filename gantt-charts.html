This week we talked about our progress, and re-evaluated the dependencies of our allocated tasks. Overall everything looks like it will be finished on target ready to be proofread before submission.

(Beginning of Week 6)

We got all the individual components ready for Tuesday so we could sit down and proofread and potentially submit. The deadline for website updates was also changed to the potential new submission date to keep in line with the rest of the work done.
